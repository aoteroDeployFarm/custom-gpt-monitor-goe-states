The following is being provided to inform the public of current environmental air matters.
Air Pollution Control Permits
For air permit applications that require a public comment period, the Administrative Record is provided below.  The Administrative Record for a permit application includes the draft Air Permit, draft Air Permit Review, and Public Notice.  To obtain copies of the Administrative Record for an application, please see the Public Notice.

The Air Permit states the conditions under which a facility is allowed to operate.
The Air Permit Review gives an assessment of the project, the regulatory requirements, and the proposed actions.
The Public Notice provides information on the public comment period, the public hearing, if applicable, and other related topics. Copies of the notice may also appear in the statewide Honolulu Star Advertiser and other local newspapers. The “closing date” represents the due date for any comments that may be solicited by the notice.
