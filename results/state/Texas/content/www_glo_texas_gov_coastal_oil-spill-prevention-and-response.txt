Oil Spill Prevention and Response
As millions of barrels of oil pass through ports, bays, and beaches along the Texas Gulf, the Texas General Land Office Oil Spill Prevention and Response team stands on call 24/7, ensuring oil stays out of Texas coastal waters.

Educate
GLO promotes boater education to keep Gulf waters clean, highlighting risks of small spills.











                  Coastal
    
Prevent

                  Coastal
    
Respond
GLO teams mitigate spills quickly, equipped with advanced tools and real-time tracking systems.

Oil Spill Response Mapping Tool





The Oil Spill Response Mapping Tool contains information from the Oil Spill Toolkit, TGLO coastal resources, and NOAA. Use the provided tools to generate reports for ICS forms, identify sensitive habitats and species, view maritime information, query spatial data, and other mapping activities.




Environmental Sensitivity Index Shoreline





Shoreline of Texas with sensitivity classification of habitats to oil contamination and removal. Classification conducted by the University of Texas Bureau of Economic Geology.
